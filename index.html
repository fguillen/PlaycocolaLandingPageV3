We Get It

Playtesting Shouldn't Be a Pain in the Neck

You want feedback, but the big platforms want your soul (and your budget), and begging friends on Discord is... well, it's a lot of work.

We built Playcocola because we were tired of the "SaaS trap." We wanted a tool that was as agile as an indie dev.

From "Build" to "Feedback" in 3 Steps:

  • Set up a Play Session. Add your game and describe your expectations for the playtesters.
  • Share with the community. Share the link with your community or tap into our pool of passionate testers. They don’t have to download a thing—it all happens in the browser.
  • Watch the "Aha!" moments. Get synced videos of their gameplay, their voice, and their comments. See exactly where they got stuck and why.

About Us

Playcocola isn't a faceless corporation. We're a small crew of devs who believe that every indie legend starts with a well-tested prototype. We're in the trenches with you, shipping updates, squashing bugs, and rooting for your Steam launch.

We believe that great games aren't born in a vacuum—they are forged in the hands of players. For too long, professional-grade playtesting has been locked behind a "corporate gate." We saw the gap between "building a game" and "understanding the player," and we decided to bridge it—not with a corporation, but with a community.
