The Playtesting Platform for the Indie Dev Community

Watching other people playing your game is the best way, if not the only way, to test your game.

Playcocola simplifies the process of collecting gameplay videos as well as feedback from your playtesters.

We know how difficult it is to find playtesters and to receive valuable feedback for your game. With Playcocola , every casual player turns into a high-quality playtester.

You are looking to test and receive feedback for your game. You do not need to have a big budget. We built Playcocola for you. Join in and start testing your game.

Indie Testing

Get Videos of People Playing your Game

Playcocola allows anyone to record their screen and upload the file with minimal effort on their side.

Starting to collect videos from your playtesters is as easy as creating a play session and sharing the invitation link with your playtesters.

  • Easy and simple setup
  • Browser-based recording app
  • No registration is needed for the playtesters
  • Automatic video uploads

Advantages of using Playcocola

We are especially focused on helping indie devs with their playtesting needs.

Easy Use

It’s Easy! Send an invitation link to your playtesters. They just have to click on it to start recording a video of their session. The video recording and comments will be sent to you automatically.

Indie Friendy

We are indie devs ourselves. We built this platform to help the specific needs that you and your team have as indie devs.

Inexpensive

We get it! Your budget matters. We feel the price of our service should be affordable to anyone. If you have special budget conditions please let us know.

About

About Us

We are a group of Indie Devs and passionate Playtesters and User Researchers. We love the indie dev community and we have created the first platform exclusively focused on helping indie devs with their playtesting needs. Come and join us at Playcocola.

Our mission is to make playtesting accessible and affordable to everyone developing a game, no matter how indie their studio is. We want to create an indie dev playtesting community where eager devs can meet bighearted playtesters and join together in the magic process of creating a game.
